Strengthening Early Childhood Development

Enhancing the Capacity of Caregivers and Improving Access to Quality Education

Program Overview

This project aims to strengthen early childhood development in Siaya, Kisumu, Homa Bay, Migori, and Nyamira counties. By enhancing the capacity of caregivers, improving access to quality early childhood education, and promoting child health and nutrition, the program seeks to ensure holistic development and better educational outcomes for children by December 2024.
