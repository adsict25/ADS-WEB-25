ADS Nyanza: A Journey of Transformation

1975 - Founding of IRDP

ACK Diocese of Maseno South launches the Integrated Rural Development Program to improve community well-being.

1981 - Establishment of PBCCS

CPK establishes the Provincial Board of Christian Community Services to guide development initiatives.

1983 - Consultative Meeting

PBCCS organizes a nationwide consultation to expand its ministry, incorporating community development initiatives.

1990 - Expansion of CCS

Christian Community Services expands its reach, integrating more dioceses into its development initiatives.

1997 - Formation of IDCCS

Diocesan Development Departments merge to create Inter-Diocesan Christian Community Services, streamlining efforts.

2010 - Rebranding to ADS Nyanza

IDCCS rebrands to ADS Nyanza, enhancing its focus on sustainable development and community empowerment.

2020 - Digital Transformation

ADS Nyanza integrates digital tools to improve service delivery and community engagement.
