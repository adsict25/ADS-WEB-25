Our Mission

ADS-Nyanza exists “To enable the Dioceses and community within Nyanza Region to achieve the fullness of life and the integrity of creation through sustainable community development programs for the glory of God”.

Our Vision

ADS-Nyanza envisions a dignified, responsible humanity enjoying the fullness of life.

Our Core Values

  • Christ Centered
  • Human Dignity
  • Compassion
  • Excellence
  • Stewardship

Our Latest Updates

Dec 12, 2024
MtM Project

Empowering 2,000 Caregivers in Early Childhood Development

The "Moments That Matter" project has successfully reached over 2,000 caregivers, ensuring children aged 0-3 years receive nurturing care, proper nutrition, and medical support.

Nov 25, 2024
CRP

Strengthening 50 Vulnerable Communities Against Climate Change

The CRP program has fortified community resilience through climate-smart agriculture, reaching over 3,500 households in climate-affected regions.

Oct 15, 2024
PESP

Digital Agriculture Training for 10,000 Farmers

Through the PESP project, over 10,000 farmers have been empowered with digital tools like iShamba and Plantix, enhancing productivity and sustainability.

Nov 5, 2024
Emergency Response

Rapid Response to 8,000 Disaster-Affected Individuals

ADS Nyanza's Emergency Response program provided shelter, food, and psychosocial support to over 8,000 people affected by recent floods and emergencies.

Oct 30, 2024
Peace Initiative

Bridging Communities: Promoting Peace in West Kenya

In 2024, the West Kenya Peace Initiative successfully mediated conflicts in 20 communities, fostering dialogue and reducing tensions through events like game activities.

Dec 5, 2024
Global Fund

Fighting Malaria: Reaching 1 Million People with Preventive Care

The Global Fund Initiative distributed over 1 million mosquito nets and conducted malaria awareness campaigns, drastically reducing infections in the region.
